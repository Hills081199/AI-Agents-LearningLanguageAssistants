    The Three Gorges attract countless tourists due to their unique natural scenery. Tall mountains stand on both sides of the gorge, which is deep and steep, making people feel the grandeur and power of nature. After the dam was completed, the river was blocked to form a high water level, which made navigation more convenient and also protected riverside cities from flood disasters. But during the dam construction, many ancient sites and villages were submerged, and part of the natural resources were affected. This reflects the conflict between modern development and environmental protection.

    Regarding tourism, the Three Gorges Scenic Area offers a variety of activities and experiences. Visitors can take cruise ships down the river, enjoying the steep cliffs and ancient trees along the banks. The guides on the boat usually introduce the history and geography of the Three Gorges, helping visitors better understand this magical land. Additionally, there are many museums and cultural exhibitions nearby showcasing the local customs and traditional crafts of the Three Gorges area.

    Overall, the Yangtze Three Gorges is a place that integrates historical culture and natural beauty. Compared with other tourist destinations, the scenery here is more magnificent and the history is longer.
