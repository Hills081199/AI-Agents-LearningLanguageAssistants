# Lesson: The symbolism and cultural significance of musical instruments in traditional Chinese puppet theatre (皮影戏) - HSK 3

## Lesson Plan

**Lesson Plan Outline: The Symbolism and Cultural Significance of Musical Instruments in Traditional Chinese Puppet Theatre (皮影戏) — HSK 3 Level**

---

**1. Learning Objectives:**  
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:  
- Understand and describe basic concepts related to traditional Chinese puppet theatre (皮影戏) and its musical instruments.  
- Recognize and use key vocabulary to talk about musical instruments and art forms.  
- Use simple sentence structures to express cultural significance and symbolism.  
- Improve listening and speaking skills by discussing the role of music in puppet theatre.  

---

**2. Key Vocabulary (HSK 3 Appropriate):**  
1. 皮影戏 (pí yǐng xì) — shadow puppet theatre  
2. 乐器 (yuè qì) — musical instrument  
3. 鼓 (gǔ) — drum  
4. 笛子 (dí zi) — flute  
5. 表演 (biǎo yǎn) — performance  
6. 文化 (wén huà) — culture  
7. 象征 (xiàng zhēng) — symbolize  
8. 传统 (chuán tǒng) — tradition  
9. 声音 (shēng yīn) — sound  
10. 美丽 (měi lì) — beautiful  

---

**3. Grammar Points:**  

**a. Expressing intuition or opinion using “觉得” (jué de)**  
- Structure: [Subject] + 觉得 + [adjective/noun/clause]  
- Example: 我觉得笛子的声音很美丽。 (I feel the flute's sound is beautiful.)  
- Focus: Students learn to express feelings or opinions about musical instruments and performances.  

**b. Using “因为...所以...” to express cause and effect**  
- Structure: 因为 + [reason], 所以 + [result]  
- Example: 因为鼓声很响亮，所以它在表演中很重要。 (Because the drum sounds very loud, it is very important in the performance.)  
- Focus: Students practice explaining why certain instruments are important in puppet theatre.  

---

**4. Suggested Activities (brief):**  
- Vocabulary matching: match words with pictures of instruments and puppet theatre scenes.  
- Listening: short audio describing the instruments used in 皮影戏.  
- Speaking: students share their opinions using “觉得”.  
- Writing: simple sentences using 因为...所以... structure to explain cultural elements.  

---

This lesson plan integrates culture, vocabulary development, and grammar suited for HSK 3 learners to engage deeply with the unique art form of traditional Chinese puppet theatre and its musical dimensions.

---

## Story

# Story (Hanzi)  
皮影戏是一种中国传统的表演艺术。皮影戏的乐器很特别，常常用鼓和笛子来伴奏。鼓的声音很响亮，能够让表演更加生动。因为鼓声很响亮，所以它在皮影戏中很重要。笛子的声音很美丽，我觉得听笛子的时候很舒服。笛子的声音象征着风和水，给皮影戏带来自然的感觉。  

除了鼓和笛子，皮影戏还有其他乐器。所有的乐器一起，能够让观众更好地理解故事的情感和气氛。皮影戏不仅是一种娱乐，它还是中国文化的重要象征。因为皮影戏有丰富的文化背景和象征意义，所以它被很多人喜欢和保护。  

我觉得学习皮影戏的音乐知识很有趣。通过了解鼓和笛子的声音和文化，我们可以更好地欣赏这种美丽的传统艺术。皮影戏让我们感觉中国文化很特别，也让音乐变得生动和有意义。  

# Pinyin  
Pí yǐng xì shì yī zhǒng Zhōngguó chuántǒng de biǎoyǎn yìshù. Pí yǐng xì de yuèqì hěn tèbié, chángcháng yòng gǔ hé dízi lái bànzòu. Gǔ de shēngyīn hěn xiǎngliàng, nénggòu ràng biǎoyǎn gèngjiā shēngdòng. Yīnwèi gǔ shēng hěn xiǎngliàng, suǒyǐ tā zài pí yǐng xì zhōng hěn zhòngyào. Dízi de shēngyīn hěn měilì, wǒ juéde tīng dízi de shíhou hěn shūfú. Dízi de shēngyīn xiàngzhēng zhe fēng hé shuǐ, gěi pí yǐng xì dàilái zìrán de gǎnjué.  

Chúle gǔ hé dízi, pí yǐng xì hái yǒu qítā yuèqì. Suǒyǒu de yuèqì yīqǐ, nénggòu ràng guānzhòng gèng hǎo de lǐjiě gùshì de qínggǎn hé qìfēn. Pí yǐng xì bùjǐn shì yī zhǒng yúlè, tā hái shì Zhōngguó wénhuà de zhòngyào xiàngzhēng. Yīnwèi pí yǐng xì yǒu fēngfù de wénhuà bèijǐng hé xiàngzhēng yìyì, suǒyǐ tā bèi hěnduō rén xǐhuan hé bǎohù.  

Wǒ juéde xuéxí pí yǐng xì de yīnyuè zhīshì hěn yǒuqù. Tōngguò liǎojiě gǔ hé dízi de shēngyīn hé wénhuà, wǒmen kěyǐ gèng hǎo de xīnshǎng zhè zhǒng měilì de chuántǒng yìshù. Pí yǐng xì ràng wǒmen gǎnjué Zhōngguó wénhuà hěn tèbié, yě ràng yīnyuè biàn dé shēngdòng hé yǒu yìyì.  

# Translation  
Shadow puppet theatre is a traditional Chinese performing art. The musical instruments used in shadow puppet theatre are very special; drums and flutes are often used to accompany the performance. The drum sounds very loud and can make the performance more vivid. Because the drum sounds very loud, it is very important in shadow puppet theatre. The sound of the flute is very beautiful; I feel very comfortable when listening to the flute. The flute’s sound symbolizes wind and water, bringing a natural feeling to the shadow puppet theatre.  

Besides drums and flutes, shadow puppet theatre also uses other musical instruments. All the instruments together help the audience better understand the emotions and atmosphere of the story. Shadow puppet theatre is not only a form of entertainment, it is also an important symbol of Chinese culture. Because shadow puppet theatre has a rich cultural background and symbolic meaning, many people like and protect it.  

I think learning about the music of shadow puppet theatre is very interesting. By understanding the sounds and culture of the drum and flute, we can better appreciate this beautiful traditional art. Shadow puppet theatre makes us feel that Chinese culture is very special and also makes music vivid and meaningful.
